What is a crypto on-ramp?

A crypto on-ramp allows users to convert fiat currency into cryptocurrency, enabling easy and direct access to the crypto market.

How do I buy cryptocurrency?

You can buy cryptocurrency via onramp by, completing KYC verification, selecting payment method, selecting the cryptocurrency to purchase, and completing the transaction.

What payment methods are supported?

You can pay using Wire transfer, Visa or Mastercard.

Can I use Zero ID for logging into websites?

Yes, you can use ZeroID for logging into websites. ZeroID allows users to prove their identity without sharing sensitive information, enhancing security and privacy during the authentication process.

Is it safe to buy cryptocurrency online?

Buying cryptocurrency online is safe if you use trusted platforms, secure your account, and avoid public Wi-Fi.
